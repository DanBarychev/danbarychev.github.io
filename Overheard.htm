HackCMU | Carnegie Mellon University

In Summary

Overheard was an iOS application that I led the development of for HackCMU 2016. The application allows you to observe and post sightings on CMU campus, similar to the Overheard at CMU Facebook group. The team consisted of me, Adrian Markelov, Kai Wen Wang, and William Qian.
