Take Me Out

Take Me Out is a social application for both iOS and Android that recommends nearby activities. It was created at PennApps XIV, with me devleloping on the iOS version, Guangyu Chen devloping the Android version, and Alexandr Rudenko providing the mathematics for the algorithms. Sara Misra worked on the logo and designs of the application.

The project recieved an honorable mention award from Lyft for its use of their API
