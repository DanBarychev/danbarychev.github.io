BACelet

During the summer of 2016, I had the privledge of working for BACelet LLC, an organization making wrist breathalyzers. My job was to create the initial iOS application that would pair to the BACelet via Bluetooth and provide pertinent information to the user, integrating Firebase to create a social media and friends systems within the application.
