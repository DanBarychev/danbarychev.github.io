LMHS Mini-THON

During my time at Lower Moreland High School, I served as a technology captain during my junior and senior years for their Mini-THON, an event which raised over $300,000 for pediatric cancer research during that time. I worked with a team of 5 to 7 other captains and many committee members to manage technical resources during the day of the event and during related events leading up to it.
