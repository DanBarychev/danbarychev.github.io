ExCITe Center

Also during the summer of 2015, I interned at the ExCITe center under the supervision of Dr. Youngmoo Kim and Matthew Prockup. I was working on a remote tap-sensitive effects trigger for acoustic instruments using optocouplers and Arduino technology. The ExCITe Center (Expressive and Creative Interaction Technologies) is a creative research space owned by Drexel Univeristy.
